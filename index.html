Working with me

Team work makes the dream work. Over the years, I gathered experience in working with people, as much as interfacing with computers.

Being a fan of the “think-before-you-act” approach, I prefer to spend some time figuring out a bigger picture of your task/problem before diving into coding. This will prevent you from lots of redos in the future, thus saving our time and your money.

Working with me, you will feel safe and involved in the process (seeing intermediate results, getting instructions, etc). It's my responsibility as a developer to free you from thinking and caring too much about things that are my responsibility.

Besides striving to be pixel-perfect and responsive, I apply best practices in order to deliver applications your users will enjoy using.
