soft skills

Team work makes the dream work. Over the years I gathered experience in working with people, as much as interfacing with computers.

Being a fan of the “think-before-you-act” approach, I prefer to spend some time figuring out a bigger picture of your task/problem before diving into coding. This will prevent you from lots of redos in the future, thus saving our time and your money.

Working with me, you will feel safe and involved in the process (seeing intermediate results, getting instructions, etc). Its my responsibility as a developer to free you from thinking and caring too much about things that are my responsibility.

  • Always stay calm and friendly, especially in high pressure situations.
  • Own responsibility instead of redirecting blame.
  • Always try to understand how you can help the person needing help from you.
  • Thank people if you get the chance to.
  • Try your best to be useful.
  • Think about the big picture and don't get lost in detail.

I believe these skills take a big part of my value as a software developer.
